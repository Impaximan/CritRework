Update 1.6.1.0
Additions:
-Added Target Practice, a new augmentation for yoyos
-Added unique crit conditions for Waterbolt, Magic Missile, and Magic Harp
-Added a config option for non-critical damage multiplier
-Added some new internal commands for other mods to interface with (which I will be using for an addon soon)

Changes:
-Backstab crit can now work with custom AI types, and can now be applied to shortswords (courtesy @Moonlight Glint)
-Amazon and Yelets now both have backstab crits by default
-Slimes are now included for backstab crits
-Nerfed Curser's Quill + cooldown crit combo

Fixes:
-Fixed NPC attacks and falling stars inheriting your current held item's crit
-Fixed Divine Ingot not correctly wiping previous prefixes stats
