Update 1.5.0.0
New additions:
-Added Fire in a Bottle, a new accessory which can be found deep underground
-Added 6 new vanity accessories which allow you to customize the sounds made by critical strikes
-Added "Special" prefixes, which are prefixes exclusive to weapons with certain critical strike conditions
-[b]This means over 80 new prefixes[/b]

Balance changes:
-Prosthetic Arm can no longer have cooldown-based crit conditions
-Reduced drop rates of Noxious Eye, Rabid Claw, and Sparking Sludge
-Reworked "Critically strikes if the projectile has been travelling for more than 1 second" slightly
-Crystal shield now has a 10 second cooldown after taking damage
-Reduced critical strike damage of the sniper rifle to account for all of the accessory additions that have been made since the mod's release (still very powerful)
-Reduced crit damage of shadowbeam staff
