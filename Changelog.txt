Update 1.4.0.0
-Added "Sharpened Wrench," a new early game accessory found in chests
-Added "Sparking Sludge," a new early game accessory dropped by slimes
-Added "Prosthetic Arm," a new early hardmode accessory crafted with Souls of Fright
-The "critically strikes while debuffed" buff now only works with damage over time debuffs
-Above foe and below foe crit conditions can now only apply to items of rarity orange or under
-Assassin's dagger now has a 15 second cooldown instead of a 10 second cooldown
-Removed "Critically strikes on targets with 3 or more debuffs" crit condition
-Nerfed "Critically strikes for 1 second after selection this weapon" crit condition

[b]Added cross mod content/balance changes with various mods[/b]

[b]~~ORCHID MOD~~[/b]
-Updated for compatibility with Orchid Mod's Shapeshifter class;
-Added 9 unique critical strike conditions for the shapeshifter class from Orchid mod
-"Close to foes" crit condition can no longer be added to shapeshifter items
-Close to foe and far away from foe crit conditions can no longer apply to guardian weapons

[b]~~THORIUM MOD~~[/b]
-Added a unique critical strike condition for bard weapons
-Added a unique critical strike condition for thrower weapons

[b]~~CALAMITY MOD~~[/b]
-Added 2 new unique critical strike conditions for when rage and adrenaline are active
-Added 1 new unique critical strike condition for rogue weapons
